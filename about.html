Our Mission

Packaging That Connects Brands to the World

At Eco Tray Solutions, our mission is simple: to make high-quality, sustainable, custom-printed paper packaging accessible to businesses of every size, in every corner of the globe. We believe that great packaging is not a luxury — it is a fundamental part of how a brand communicates its values to the world.

Founded by a team of packaging industry veterans and international trade specialists, we have built the supplier relationships, logistics networks, and licensing infrastructure required to operate seamlessly across borders. Today, we serve clients in over 50 countries — from independent cafés to large hospitality groups — delivering a complete, end-to-end service that removes every barrier between an idea and a finished, delivered product.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a local packaging idea to a globally licensed operation serving clients across five continents.

Year 1
🌱

Founded with a Vision

Eco Tray Solutions was established with a clear goal: to bridge the gap between high-quality overseas manufacturers and businesses that needed reliable, affordable custom packaging. Our first clients were local food and beverage operators looking for a genuine alternative to generic, unbranded bags. We shipped our first order within weeks and never looked back.

Year 2
🌍

International Expansion

Having secured our first set of import/export licences, we expanded operations to serve clients across multiple continents. We built partnerships with certified print factories and established our dedicated quality-control process — ensuring every single shipment met exacting standards before leaving the factory floor.

Year 3
🌿

Eco-First Product Line

We transitioned our entire product catalogue to FSC-certified, biodegradable paper materials. This was not a marketing decision — it was a values-driven one. Our clients increasingly wanted packaging that reflected their own sustainability commitments, and we agreed that responsible business should be the baseline, not the exception.

Today
🚀

50+ Countries & Growing

We now serve over 500 clients worldwide across kraft bags, restaurant menus, marketing stickers, and bespoke packaging projects. Our door-to-door logistics model — combined with full global licensing — makes us one of the most complete and trusted packaging partners available to international businesses today. And we are just getting started.
